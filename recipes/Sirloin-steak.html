Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients and preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the gate.
  2. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and garlic powder and set aside.
  4. Season both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the seasoned steaks on the preheated grill and cook 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 60 degrees C (140 degrees F) for medium doneness.
  6. Transfer steaks to warmed plates; brush the tops liberally with garlic butter and allow to rest for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
